Mr.Maheshkumar P ( MK)
Founder & Chief Advocate

Mr.P.Maheshkumar (MK) is our founder, chief advocate and sole proprietor of our firm M/s. Wallcliffs Law firm. We specialise on all civil, criminal, labour, tax, banking, corporate, family laws and all other laws required for corporates, start up, developing and developed business firms in one roof. Our founder MK started our firm way back in 2003 and with his rich experience in legal arena over span of 17 years, he made our firm to earn its credibility among good many domestic and international clients.

Our firm has its offices at Chennai, Coimbatore and Tirupur which are taken care by its highly skilled and determined associate attorneys and attorneys guided by our Chief advocate. We also have associated Offices in major state capitals and abroad.
